Our Journey

Crafting Legacies in the
Heart of Assam

Born from Tradition

Founded in Guwahati, Saavan Heritage Events began with a simple observation: modern celebrations were losing their soulful connection to the earth and our ancestors. We set out to bridge the gap between contemporary luxury and the raw, rhythmic beauty of Northeast Indian traditions.

Whether it's the intricate weaving of a Muga Mekhela Sador or the sacred chanting of a Vedic Mahurat, we believe that the details of our heritage are what make a wedding truly unforgettable. We don't just plan events; we preserve stories.

01.

Authentic Artistry

Collaborating exclusively with local artisans, weavers, and folk performers to keep our culture alive.

02.

Soulful Luxury

Blending high-end production with the quiet, intentional grace of traditional Assamese hospitality.

03.

Sustainable Roots

Using locally sourced florals and hand-crafted decor to ensure every celebration respects the land.

A Message from Saavan

"I remember watching my grandmother prepare for a Joron ceremony—the meticulous care, the specific placement of the tamul-pan, the quiet reverence for the ritual. That sense of 'purpose' is what I bring to every wedding we design today. My mission is to ensure that while your wedding is grand, its soul remains deeply rooted in the soil of Assam."

Founder & Creative Director
